Welcome to viewdu portals.

This site is a blank installation of the portal application demonstrating the core functionality.

These include:

  • On premise, Hosted or Software as a Service (SaaS) installation
  • Extendable modular content where your solutions become subscribable SaaS offerings
  • Full user self-service experience
    • Self-serve subscription cart with credit card payment
    • Account authentication with email verification
    • Social media sites (e.g.: Login with Facebook) authentication
    • Site content authentication and authorization
    • User-based forgotten password reset (with email/SMS alert)
    • Configurable user-feedback tracking and reporting
    • In-built reporting and business intelligence
